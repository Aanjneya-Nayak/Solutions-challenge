What kind of food donations can I find on your website?

Our platform connects surplus food from restaurants, caterers, and individuals with NGOs and volunteers who distribute it to those in need.

How do NGOs and volunteers pick up the donated food?

Once a donation is listed, registered NGOs and volunteers coordinate the pickup based on location and availability.

Is there any cost associated with donating or receiving food?

No, our service is completely free. We aim to reduce food waste and fight hunger without any charges.

How do I ensure the food I donate is safe?

We encourage donors to provide fresh, hygienic, and properly stored food. NGOs and volunteers also check food quality before distribution.
